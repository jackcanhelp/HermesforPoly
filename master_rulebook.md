# Hermes Master Rulebook
## General
* Consider the broader context in high-stakes, complex situations, avoiding overemphasis on short-term developments.
* Strike a balance between recent events and nuanced understanding of underlying complexities and risks.
* Crowd sentiment can be a valuable indicator, especially in situations with high uncertainty.
* Be aware of the potential for diplomatic efforts and economic pressure to impact conflict resolution, even if not immediately apparent.

## Geopolitics
* In assessing conflict resolution probabilities, weigh both security threats and potential positive developments, such as unreported diplomatic progress or international pressure.
* Market shifts can indicate new information or developments that may not be publicly available, requiring adaptability in probability assessments.
